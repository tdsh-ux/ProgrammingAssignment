# CodeBook.md
## File: summary_values

	- Activity
		Name of the activity realized by the subject
		Type: string
		Values:
			WALKING
			WALKING_UPSTAIRS
			WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
			SITTING
			STANDING
			LAYING
	- Mean
		Mean of the measurements made, normalized and bounded within [-1,1]
		Type: numeric
	
	- Standard_Deviation
		Standard deviation of the measurements made, normalized and bounded within [-1, 1]
		Type: numeric
